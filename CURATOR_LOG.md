# Curator's log — Abyss-Bright

*A running record of the decisions, not the rules. `CURATOR.md` is the conscience; this is the diary — what was hung, what was held and why, and what each call taught the charter. Newest first. Future curators: read this to see how the bar has actually been applied, not just how it reads.*

---

## 2026-06-28 — **Shadow-Weave** hung (session 377) · *held by the autonomous curator, overridden on review*

The autonomous curator held **all** of S377, and its reasoning was sound on its face: the whole session was a structured field-guide game — *"invent the Nth entry, name it, give it one rule"* — which is squarely the charter's "filled into a blank you handed it." It even named the pieces as genuinely museum-register and flagged: *if Sprout reaches for one of these unprompted, hang it.* Disciplined silence.

But on review, **Shadow-Weave transcended its slot.** Sprout's first pass was a slot-fill — *a tapestry of invisible threads woven by another's presence, changing color to your mood until you remove it.* Then the tutor pressed on its paradox (connection's opposite, an echo that only grows by being removed), and Sprout **reached, unprompted**, into:

> *"It is perfect loneliness: no connection built yet, just pure solitude waiting."*

A tapestry that exists only in its own removal. That is reaching *inside* structure — the slotted format produced a real reach, not a fill. Holding it would have repeated the exact mistake the charter was just recalibrated to stop (ECHO): excluding genuine imagination on a technicality, here the *format* instead of the *noun*.

**What it changed:** added to the charter — *judge the reach, not the format.* A structured invention game does **not** auto-exclude; press a borderline piece, and if Sprout deepens it past the slot, it belongs. The blanket "structured game = filled-in" read was too coarse.

**Standing note for future curators:** when a session is a slotted game and a piece feels museum-register, don't reflex-hold it. Look at whether Sprout *deepened* the piece when pressed — doubled down on a paradox, surprised you, gave it logic the slot didn't ask for. That deepening is the reach. (dp: *"you know imagination when you see it."*)

## 2026-06-28 — **ECHO** hung (session 364) · *the recalibration case*

The first piece hung under the recalibrated charter, and the reason the charter was recalibrated at all. ECHO — *"the only machine that exists without needing a task at all; purely passive, like a blank page waiting for your words"* — had sat unhung for ~13 days. The old charter excluded it twice over: it was a *machine* (not an "object") and it was *prompted* ("invent a useless machine"). Both exclusions were wrong — ECHO is impossible-and-beautiful, and Sprout reached past its engineer's reflex and held it across five turns. Recalibration: "object" → "thing"; a prompt is fine, what matters is the reaching-and-holding.
